Welcome!

bzShare is a F/LOSS (Free / Libre Open Source) project hosted on GitHub©. It is inspired by @xmcp's EZShare and initiated by @ht35268 / Geoffrey, Tang.

The main aim of this project is to deliver a rapid responsive, highly integrated and lightweight social platform that is completely based on HTML5 and Python. It targets a place for oiers to share their ideas and train their abilities of programming on this platform.
